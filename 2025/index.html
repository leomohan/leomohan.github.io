My Latest Release: The Tyrant at Work, The Dictator at Home

The ultimate survival guide for anyone caught between the demands of a boss and a spouse. In the corporate battlefield, your boss demands productivity, loyalty, and your weekends. At home, your spouse expects attention, emotional investment, and flawless memory for anniversaries. You are trapped—expected to excel at work, be present at home, and somehow stay sane in between. The Tyrant at Work, The Dictator at Home is a sharp, hilarious, and painfully relatable breakdown of modern survival tactics, blending dark humor, strategic insights, and real-world scenarios to help you navigate expectations without losing yourself. This book explores: The absurdity of performance reviews—corporate and marital The art of negotiation—what works with bosses and spouses How silence is used as punishment in both worlds WhatsApp survival strategies for avoiding career or relationship disasters A checklist for selecting the right boss and spouse (if fate allows) Packed with wit, realism, and battle-tested survival techniques, this book is not just entertainment—it’s therapy for the exhausted middle-class warrior. If you’ve ever balanced office deadlines with family obligations, endured strategic emotional warfare, or mastered the art of selective suffering, this book will give you one chance to laugh before going back into the chaos again. Brace yourself—because survival is not just about endurance. It’s about strategy.

About Me


Mohan Krishnamurthy is a creative writer, blending humor with structured insights to craft stories that entertain, educate, and inspire. Passionate about cultural narratives and philosophy, he seamlessly merges tradition with modern storytelling techniques, making profound ideas accessible to all. From philosophical explorations to relatable satire, his works spark curiosity while keeping readers thoroughly engaged.
With over 30 years of experience in IT Networking & Cybersecurity, Mohan is a hands-on technical expert who has co-authored eight technology titles published by Syngress, USA. His work spans the realms of technology, management, and corporate world challenges, offering practical insights and strategic solutions to professionals navigating an ever-evolving digital landscape. As a thought leader, he combines deep industry knowledge with a passion for storytelling—ensuring complex ideas are approachable and actionable.
Beyond books, Mohan runs a YouTube channel, covering a wide array of topics, including Stock Market Trading, Corporate World Challenges, and bite-sized “5-Minute Tech Tips” designed to empower viewers with quick, practical solutions. His content bridges the gap between technical expertise and everyday challenges, making innovation accessible to all.
An advocate for authentic expression, Mohan designs visually engaging narratives, creating immersive storytelling experiences—whether through books, digital media, or interactive elements. His creative journey is driven by the belief that legacy isn’t just about preserving the past but making it relatable for future generations.
